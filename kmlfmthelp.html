USGS KML Formatter - Help

The FDM converter changes legacy .fd files into .csv files. The .csv files can easily be read by Excel and formatted further at that point.


Example .fd file opened up in WordPad

Instructions:

1. Go to the FDM Converter and browse for your .fd file.
2. Click the 'Convert' button.
3. Save the .csv output. Open it up in Excel or WordPad to verify the results.
