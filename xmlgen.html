Vertical Extent (Optional): ?Vertical extent information can be provided and is especially useful for rock core samples. Specification of extent can contain three elements: minimum value, maximum value, and unit of measure. These elements will be collected as 2 or 3 values representing the UnitOfMeasure and MaximumValue with the possible addition of MinimumValue (e.g., m,35.4,0 for a rock core measured at 35.4 meters).
