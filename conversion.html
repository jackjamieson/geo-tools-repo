Conversion Tools

The conversion tools are web based utilities that help move between various formats.

Field Data Management (FDM) to CSV
Convert legacy .FD files made with the Field Data Management software to ESRI readable .TXT files with proper dip/dip azimuth.

NJ Atlas Sheet Coordinates to NAD27
Convert Atlas Sheets into DDMMSS.SS, Decimal Degrees, and USGS Quadrangle (NAD27). Also goes from Lat/Long back to Atlas Sheet and USGS Quadrangle. It supports batch Atlas Sheet conversions and can export for NADCON or KML (batch only works in Chrome). See also the help file for this tool.

DDMMSS and Decimal Degree Converter
Converts between DDMMSS and Decimal Degree accurate to 3 decimal places in NAD27.
