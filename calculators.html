Calculators

These tools will take copy-pasted data from Excel spreadsheets and perform various geologic calculations.

Lineation Calulator
Take in plane dip azimuth, dip, and lineation pitch.

Apparent Dip Calculator
Calculates the apparent dip given the true dip, cross section azimuth, and plane dip azimuth.

Trend and Plunge from Pitch Calculator
Calculates the trend and plunge given the pitch and dip.
